Project Showcase

This page highlights a selection of my Textiles and Food Technology projects. Each project demonstrates planning, creativity and practical skills that I aim to foster in my future students. Click on any image to view a larger version.

Textiles – Matching Pyjama Set

A textiles project involving the design and construction of a matching pyjama set. The project focused on pattern use, fabric choice, comfort and accurate sewing techniques.

Food Technology – Burrito Bowl

A practical food product developed as part of a Food Technology unit. The burrito bowl task focused on nutrition, flavour balance, presentation and timing in the kitchen.

Textiles– Petite Quilt

Different shibori and dyeing techniques were used to colour the fabric. Various inks were used to print the original screen prints using different colours, foil and puff to add colour and texture.

Industrial: Timber – Laser-cut Lamp

A lamp designed and produced using laser-cut plywood. The cut pattern creates interesting shadows when the lamp is switched on, linking design decisions with function and atmosphere.

Textiles – Felt Banner

A decorative banner created using layered felt, applique and hand-stitching. The design combines colour, pattern and texture to communicate a theme visually.

Food Technology – Biscoff Scrolls

A product redevelopment project where an existing baked item was redesigned. The focus was on improving sensory qualities, consistency and packaging to better meet the needs of the target market.
