University Projects

These are a collection of projects I have completed over the last 3 years

Matching pyjama set

This textiles project involved producing a screen printed design onto a tshirt. A printed cotton poplin fabric was chosen to sew a pair of pyjama pants. The same fabric was used to make a matching bag that could be used to place the pyjamas in. The set was a mermaid theme with a mermaid scale printed fabric and a mermaid tail was screen printed onto the tshirt.

Banner

This textiles banner utilised various felting and hand stitching techniques. I continued on from the mermaid pyjamas theme and created an under the sea themed banner.3 LED ligts were incorporated throughout the banner (they are not turned on in this image). 1 of the lights is placed in the middle of the sea shell to represent the pearl. The other 2 lights are placed in the mermaid tail to represent the reflection of the sunlight on the scales.

Lamp

This lamp features laser cut plywood with a tree design cut out. When the lamp is turned on, the light projects the design onto the wall.

Burrito Bowl

This was a food styling and photography task. Various tools were used to prepare the ingredients and create colour contrast. The lighting was set up to enhance the vibrant colours of each ingredient.

Biscoff Scrolls

This task involved redesigning an existing product that required improvement. I redesigned biscoff hot cross buns
