Hello!

My name is Ellie Fourfouris. I am currently in my third year of studying a Bachelor of Education (Secondary) / Bachelor of Arts (Design Innovation and Technologies) at Australian Catholic University. I am majoring in Food Technology and minoring in Textiles.

I am passionate about food, cooking and educating young minds about the significance of food and the various ways it contributes to our lives. By combining theory with hands-on experiences, I encourage students to apply their knowledge in meaningful ways, developing both technical skills and critical thinking.

I view Food Technology as an opportunity to empower students with lifelong skills—such as food preparation, nutrition literacy and safe work practices that enhance personal wellbeing and contribute to community health.
